ABOUT THE DIRECTORY

The Gulf Oilfield Directory is the most comprehensive reference guide for Products and Services to Oil and Gas industry in the Middle-East region since 1980. It is a powerful oil and gas directory encompassed with all relevant information about almost all companies and establishments serving in the Oil, Gas, Energy and Petrochemical industry in the Gulf region.

The directory carries the names of the companies and their profiles with other relevant details and this has made it an invaluable and indispensible reference medium to the Oil and Gas Industry. The directory is widely subscribed and has most user friendly listing of products, services, buyers and suppliers in the Oil and Gas industry worldwide.

Accuracy of data and quantity
Gulf Oilfield Directory is encompassed with a validated list of more than 18,000 companies included from U.A.E, BAHRAIN, KUWAIT, OMAN, QATAR, SAUDI ARABIA, IRAN, IRAQ, YEMEN, HOUSTON & other countires icluded in INTERNATIONAL sections. Each and every company detail in the directory is validated and updated annually.

Circulation
Gulf Oilfield Directory has a total world-wide circulation of 70,000 copies annually to all ,major oil companies, local and regional establishments in the Middle East, bonafide trade visitors in OTC in Houston, GASTECH in London, Offshore Europe in Aberdeen, ADIPEC in Abu Dhabi, MEOS in Bahrain, OGS in Dubai and SAOGE in KSA, local Houston distribution, international distribution including IRAN, IRAQ & YEMEN, through online request through www.oilandgaspages.com , etc. (The break-up of circulation can be provided on request).

More Features
Gulf Oilfield Directory provides a classification list of more than 700 classifications related to the Products & Services in the Oil & Gas market and the companies listed in the directory are given two or three headings as their major business whereby each company is listed under each classification in alphabetical sequence and advertisers with page numbers.

Gulf Oilfield Directory includes:
  1. ISO Certification Bodies:

    A section to find the authorized Standard Certification Bodies to certify ISO.


  2. ISO Certified Companies:

    A section for the companies those who hold ISO certification to be referred as reliable and committed.


  3. Brand Finder:

    Major Brands, used in Oil & Gas Industry, are listed with the Brand logo, details and specifications along with their local agents contact information.


  4. Classification Section:

    All the companies listed in the directory are given two or three headings as their major business whereby each company is listed under each classification in alphabetical sequence and advertisers with page numbers.


  5. Alphabetical Section:

    All the companies are listed under Alphabetical section, country-wise in an Alphabetical sequence with company details, activities, communication address, senior personal contact etc.
